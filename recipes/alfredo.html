Alfredo Recipe

The absolute best meal for the night before a big race...or if you're just flat broke!

One of the most basic, easy to put together meals for those nights the kid's baseball games run late and you gotta string up something for dinner, and quick!

With classic alfredo, you can't go wrong. It's cheesy, it's filling, and most of all, it's cheap!


Ingredients:

  • Fettuccine (Or any kind of noodles you prefer!)
  • Alfredo sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Instructions:

  1. Boil the noodles according to the package instructions
  2. Drain the pasta (may consider leaving some water to loosen sauce)
  3. Add sauce to noodles and stir
  4. Top it all off with parmesan cheese
  5. Enjoy!
